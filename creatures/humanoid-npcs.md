# Humanoid NPC Reference

Guidelines for creating level-appropriate human and near-human combatants.

## Level 1-2 NPCs (Smugglers, Bandits, Thugs)

### Quick Stats
- **Hits:** 30-50 (Base 25 + ~5-25 Body Dev bonus)
- **AT:** 3-5 (Soft Leather to Hard Leather)
- **Melee OB:** +30 to +50 (depending on weapon and training)
- **Ranged OB:** +25 to +45
- **DB:** +5 to +15 (Quickness only, most won't have dodge or parry training)
- **Initiative:** 2d10 + 0 to +3
- **Parry:** Allocate 10-20 OB to parry if experienced; untrained thugs won't parry

### Typical Smuggler
- **Level:** 1-2
- **Hits:** 35-45
- **AT:** 4 (Hide Scale) or 5 (Hard Leather)
- **Weapon:** Arming Sword or Short Sword (+35-45 OB)
- **Ranged:** Light Crossbow (+30-40 OB) or thrown dagger (+25-35)
- **DB:** 6-12 (Qu only)
- **Initiative:** 2d10 + 1 to +2
- **RR:** Physical +2 to +5, Fear +0 to +3

### Smuggler Leader (Reef  Level 3-4)
- **Level:** 3-4
- **Hits:** 55-70
- **AT:** 5 (Hard Leather) or 6 (Rigid Leather)
- **Weapon:** Falchion or Broadsword (+55-65 OB)
- **Backup:** Hand crossbow or thrown daggers (+40-50)
- **DB:** 15-21 (Qu + maybe passive dodge)
- **Initiative:** 2d10 + 3 to +5
- **Parry:** Will allocate 20-30 OB to parry
- **RR:** Physical +5 to +10, Fear +3 to +6
- **Special:** May have Ambush skill (+20-30), basic Stalking

## Level 3-4 NPCs (Veteran Guards, Mercenaries)
- **Hits:** 50-75
- **AT:** 6-8 (Rigid Leather to Mail)
- **Melee OB:** +50 to +70
- **DB:** +15 to +25 (may include passive dodge or shield)
- **Initiative:** 2d10 + 2 to +5
- **Parry:** Will allocate 15-25 OB to parry

## Level 5+ NPCs (Elite Fighters, Knights)
- **Hits:** 70-100+
- **AT:** 8-10 (Mail to Plate)
- **Melee OB:** +65 to +90
- **DB:** +25 to +50 (shield + parry)
- **Initiative:** 2d10 + 3 to +7

## Combat Behavior
- **Untrained thugs:** Full offense, no parry, flee at 50% hits lost
- **Trained smugglers:** Moderate parry (15-20), fight until leader falls
- **Veterans:** Tactical parry (20-30), use terrain, coordinate attacks
- **Leaders:** Smart parry, target weak party members, rally allies
