# Movement and Pace Rules Reference

## Base Movement Rate (BMR)
- BMR = 20'/round + half Quickness bonus (round up) + racial stride modifier
- Grond: 28' (Qu +4, stride +6')
- Ambrosia: 26' (Qu +9, stride +1')
- Ralhann: 26' (Qu +11, stride +0')
- Rian: 22' (Qu +10, stride -3')

## Pace Table

| Pace | Distance/Round | Distance/Phase | Penalty or AP Cost | Load Limit |
|------|---------------|----------------|-------------------|------------|
| Creep |  BMR |  BMR | None |  |
| Walk | 1 BMR |  BMR | -25 or 1 AP | 90% |
| Jog | 2 BMR |  BMR | -50 or 2 AP | 60% |
| Run | 3 BMR |  BMR | -75 or 3 AP | 45% |
| Sprint | 4 BMR | 1 BMR | 4 AP (no actions) | 30% |
| Dash | 5 BMR | 1.25 BMR | 4+ AP (uses instantaneous action) | 15% |

## Key Rules
- Backwards movement: half speed, maximum Jog pace
- Pace penalty applies to ALL actions taken while moving at that pace
- Footwork skill reduces pace penalty for melee and thrown weapon attacks ONLY
- A character can spend AP on movement OR take a pace penalty  both give the same result in most cases
- Sprint and Dash use all AP for movement; no other actions possible

## Action Points (AP)
- 4 AP per round (standard)
- Actions resolved when enough AP spent
- Full action: 4 AP, no penalty
- Hasty action: 3 AP = -25 penalty; 2 AP = -50 penalty
- Drawing a weapon: 1 AP (0 AP with Quickdraw talent)
- Picking up a dropped item: 3 AP
- Dropping an item: 0 AP (instantaneous)

## Charging
- Must move at Jog or faster in a straight line toward target
- Charge bonus: +15 to melee OB
- If at Run pace or faster and larger than target: additional +1 size to attack
- Charging reduces DB by the pace penalty amount
