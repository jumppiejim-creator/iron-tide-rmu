# Spellcasting Rules Reference

## SCR Formula
SCR = d100 (open-ended) + List Ranks + Realm Stat Bonus + List Type + Other Modifiers

### List Type Modifier
- Own Base list: +5
- Open list (own realm): +0
- Closed list (own realm): -5
- Other list: -10

### Realm Stats
- Channeling: Intuition (In)
- Essence: Empathy (Em)
- Mentalism: Presence (Pr)

### Success Threshold
- **1 or greater = success**
- 0 = spell fizzles, PP lost, no failure roll
- Negative = spell failure, roll on Spell Failure table

### SCR Result Use
- The SCR result becomes the target number for enemy Resistance Rolls
- Higher SCR = harder for enemies to resist

## PP Cost
- Each spell costs PP equal to the spell's level
- PP are spent when the spell is cast, regardless of success or failure

## PP Recovery
- 10% of total PP per 2 hours of sleep
- Full recovery requires full night of sleep (8 hours)
- Efficient Sleeper: proportionally faster recovery in less sleep time

## Spell Preparation
- +10 to SCR per round of preparation (max +20, so max 2 rounds)
- Preparation = concentration (doubles AP cost of other actions during prep)

## Concentration
- Required to maintain certain spells on other targets
- Doubles the AP cost of all other actions while concentrating
- Self-cast spells typically do NOT require concentration

## Spell AP Cost
- Normal casting: 4 AP (no penalty)
- Hasty casting: 3 AP = -25; 2 AP = -50
- Quick-Caster talent: reduces AP cost for one specific spell list

## Overcasting
- Casting a spell above your own level: -20 per level above caster level
- Grace skill (specialized per list) reduces this penalty

## Directed Spell Attacks
- Cast the spell (SCR must succeed)
- Then make a separate attack roll: d100 open-ended + Directed Spell OB - target DB
- Resolve on the appropriate attack table (Lightning Bolt, Fire Bolt, Ice Bolt, etc.)
- Both the SCR and the attack roll happen in the same action

## Armor and Spellcasting
- Essence and Channeling: penalized by armor encumbrance percentage
- Mentalism: penalized only by helms
- Transcendence skill reduces armor casting penalties
