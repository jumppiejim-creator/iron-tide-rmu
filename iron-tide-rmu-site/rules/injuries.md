# Injuries and Recovery Reference

## Critical Result Notation
- **O+X**: Additional X hits of damage
- **X (bleed icon)**: Bleed X hits per round
- **-X**: Injury penalty (applies to all actions)
- **X[-YY]**: Stunned for X rounds at -YY penalty
- **(stagger icon)**: Staggered (lose 1 AP this round)
- **(prone icon)**: Knocked prone
- **X'**: Knocked back X feet
- **(grapple icon) X%**: Grappled at X%
- **(-X)**: Fatigue penalty
- **(-X) (breakage icon)**: Breakage roll at -X
- **(skull icon)**: Target is dying

## Hit Loss Penalties
| Hits Lost (%) | Penalty |
|---------------|---------|
| 25% | -5 |
| 50% | -10 |
| 75% | -20 |
| 90% | -30 |
| 100%+ | Unconscious |

## Bleeding
- Hits lost per round during Upkeep Phase
- Multiple bleeds stack
- Bleeding continues until treated (Medicine maneuver) or character dies
- Death occurs at negative hits exceeding Body Development skill bonus

## Stun
- Stunned combatants suffer the listed penalty to all actions
- +20 OB bonus when attacking a stunned foe
- Multiple stun effects: reduce the most severe first during Upkeep
- Stun reduced by 1 round per Upkeep Phase (if combatant spent less than 2 AP before being stunned)

## Prone
- -50 OB penalty while prone
- +30 OB bonus for attackers in melee against prone target
- -30 OB penalty for ranged attacks against prone target
- Standing up: 2 AP

## Staggered
- Lose 1 AP in the round staggered
- Removed during Upkeep Phase

## Death
- Character dies when negative hits exceed Body Development skill bonus
- Grond dies at -67 hits, Ambrosia at -58, Ralhann at -44, Rian at -15
