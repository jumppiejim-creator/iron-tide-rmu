## TACTICAL NOTES

- **Flanking is everything:** +15 OB for flank, +35 for rear, and Ambush bonus (+48) adds to critical rolls when attacking from flank/rear positions. Get behind them while Grond holds the front.
- **Footwork (+30):** Reduces pace penalty when moving and attacking. At a Jog (-50 pace), Footwork reduces it to -20. Essential for hit-and-reposition combat.
- **Passive Dodge (+7):** Always on, no AP cost, stacks with Quickness DB. Armor penalties fully negated by MiA.
- **Quickdraw:** Start a tavern fight with rapier already in hand. Also useful for weapon-switching mid-combat.
- **Hand Crossbow:** 1H to fire, but 6 AP to reload (more than 1 round). Treat as an opening shot, then switch to rapier. Point-blank (+20) within 10' for an ambush opener.
- **Initiative +9:** This character almost always goes first. Use that to set up flanking before enemies can react.
